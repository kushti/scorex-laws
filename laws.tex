\title{Checking Laws of the Blockchain With Property-Based Testing}

%\author{Alexander Chepurnoy \and Mayank}

\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
Inspired by success of Bitcoin, many alternative implementations of the Bitcoin protocol and of alternative blockchain protocols are implemented. However, implementations contain errors, and a cost of an error in case of a cryptocurrency could be extremely high. 

We propose to tackle the problem with a suite of abstract property tests which checks whether a blockchain system satisfies laws all the blockchain and blockchain-like systems do satisfy. To test a new blockchain system, its developers need to instantiate generators of random objects to be used by the tests. The test suite then checks the satisfaction of the laws over many random cases. We provide examples of the laws in the paper.
\end{abstract}
